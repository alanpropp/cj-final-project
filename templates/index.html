Data sources and information
  • Data for primary energy consumption comes from Data.gov , and runs from 1980-2008
  • Data for annual electricity generation comes from Data.gov , and runs from 1980-2009
  • Data for annual renewable electricity generation comes from Data.gov , and runs from 1980-2009
  • Data for natural gas consumption comes from Data.gov , and runs from 1980-2009
  • Data for biofuels consumption and production comes from Data.gov , and runs from 2000-2010 (due to the fact that biofuels are just relatively recently produced in large quantities)
  • Data for coal consumption comes from Data.gov , and runs from 1980-2012
  • Data for population comes from Data.gov , and runs from 1980-2012
